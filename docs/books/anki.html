Anki

Anki is a customizable flashcard app that uses spaced repetition to help learners efficiently memorize and retain information, including Japanese vocabulary, kanji, and grammar. Its flexibility allows users to download pre-made decks or create their own tailored to specific learning goals. Anki is especially popular among serious language learners for its effectiveness in long-term retention and daily review.
