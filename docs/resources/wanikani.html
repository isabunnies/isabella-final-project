WaniKani

WaniKani is an online tool designed to help learners master over 2,000 kanji and 6,000 vocabulary words using a spaced repetition system. It focuses on efficient memorization through mnemonic aids and timed reviews that reinforce long-term retention. Best suited for learners who want to build strong reading skills, WaniKani is a powerful supplement to any Japanese study routine.
