WaniKani

WaniKani is a spaced repetition tool to help you master over 2,000 kanji! The user interface is really intuitive, making learning kanji colorful and fun. It also helps you memorize kanji through the mnemonics system, which arguably is one of the best ways to memorize each character faster. Native Japanese speakers learn kanji from ages 4 to 18, and WaniKani does a great job of making the year-long memorization process easy and accessible. The only downside is it costs around $10 a month, and the pace is quite slow as it prioritizes spaced repetition.
