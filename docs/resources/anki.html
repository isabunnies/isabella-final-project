Anki

Anki is arguably the most important resource on this list. Learning Japanese grammar is one thing, but the most arduous task is learning vocab, especially with it’s correlating kanji. Japanese learners swear by Anki for its spaced repetition system, maximizing your memorization to its fullest potential. To truly utilize Anki, I suggest making a list of any new Japanese words you encounter throughout the week and input them into your flashcard deck. To many Japanese learners online, this is the tried and true way of getting over one of the hardest parts of learning Japanese.
